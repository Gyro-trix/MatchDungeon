As the warrior, you must use collect the symbols in the level in a specific order to open the door to the next level.

To move the warrior, use either the on screen arrow buttons to the left or the arrow keys on the keyboard.

Pressing the CTRL key on the keyboard or the A button to the right will cause the warrior to attack. Attacking allows you to destroy the red enemies in the level.
